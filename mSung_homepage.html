MotoGP Fan Club


Welcome to MotoGP Fan Club!!

Here you can learn about the most exciting motorcycle racing sport in the world,
the riders, the motorbikes, and the race tracks.

MotoGP bikes are purpose-built racing machines that are generally unavailable
for purchase by the general public or able to be ridden legally on public roads. They are hand built using rare parts, expensive materials, and comprise of the latest electronics. No wonder a MotoGP race bike can cost up to $2 Million U.S. dollars.

AMotoGP season comprises 19 Grands Prix, with 12 held in Europe, four in Asia,
two in the Americas, and one in Oceania.



The Riders

Here you will learn who the riders are, their records, and their history. Some MotoGP racers even earn over $10 million a year!
